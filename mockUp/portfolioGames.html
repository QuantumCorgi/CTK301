Bugged and Sorted

Bugged and Sorted is a sorting game where you the player work in a factory that sorts strange bug cyborg creatures, and its your job to put them in the correct place or get FIRED! This game is the result of the one week long Game Development Club Winter Game Jam that I competed in along-side my friends. It was my first ever game jam and it taught me how to work in a small group of people, and how to work under pressure. Overall our project won second place, so I'm very pround of the work we we able to accomplish!



My Work

My job was to make envirnmental assets for the game. Such as, the floor, walls, room decor, etc. I knew we were going for a facorty setting, so I went about making the assets very bland, or even covered in dirt. My favorite asset I made for Bugged and Sorted, is the Andy the Aphid poster. It adds some fun world building to the game and hammers home the "you are a cog in the machine" prompt of the game jam.
